Idea #1: Drinking enough water and eating healthy

Now, this may sound like slapping a band-aid over a larger issue, but drinking enough water and eating healthy actually can help level your moods, which can make it easier to handle stresssful situations.

Idea #2: Find a new hobby

Many people often find themselves bored, or tired with there routine that theve been doing for years. A healthy change to that routine, like picking up a hobby or trying something new, can easily add some joy into your life that was missing.

Talking to someone

People are always scared to talk about how they truly feel on the inside. It is a scary thing to open up to someone about things that the listener may not be able to relate to. The fear of judgment is also a reason why people wouldnt want to speak up about there issues. You will never be able to change whats bothering you without acceptance and actions to change it. Speaking to a trusted friend or therapist could make you feel like a weight has been lifted from the burden thats been carried.

Taking breaks and Time Away

Many people do not realize how helpful mental health days are, or just taking time away for yourself to "recharge". Make time for yourself. Take a day, or even just a few hours of your time to do what makes you feel your best. Wether thats cleaning your room, watching a movie, or ditching social media for a while. These things can help take away from some of the stressful environments in your life.
