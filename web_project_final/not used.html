Become a Better You: Services

Services that are here to help you

  • Crisis hotline - 988


  • National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI) - 1-800-950-NAMI (6264)


  • National Center for Learning Disabilities - 1-888-575-7373


  • Teenline - 310-855-4673 or text TEEN to 839863 (teens helping teens)


These websites and hotlines are just some of the thousands of hotlines and websites that address all kinds of issues. If you find yourself struggling mentally, these resources can help you if you need extra help apart from this site. Its okay to reach out and find help. These sites have helped thousands of people live better lives, so they can help you too.
